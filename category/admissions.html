About Admissions

Requirements

The following requirements must be submitted to the Registrar's Office before admission to any academic program:

Grade 11

  • Original Form 138/SF9-JHS (Learner’s Progress Report Card)
  • Original Form 137/SF10-JHS (Learner’s Permanent Academic Record)
  • PSA-issued Birth Certificate
  • Original Copy of Certificate of Good Moral Character or Recommendation from the School Principal
  • Medical Certificate

Freshmen

  • Original Form 138 / SF9-SHS (Learner's Progress Report Card)
  • Original Form 137 / SF10-SHS (Learner's Permanent Academic Record)
  • PSA-issued Birth Certificate
  • Original Copy of Certificate of Good Moral Character or Recommendation from the School Principal
  • Medical Certificate
Transferees
  • Certificate of Transfer (Honorable Dismissal)
  • Official Transcript of Records
  • PSA-issued Birth Certificate
  • Original Copy of Certificate of Good Moral Character or Recommendation from the Dean/Program or Department Head
  • Medical Certificate

Online-Pre Registration
  1. Visit the provided link to accomplish the online application form admission.pnc.edu.ph
  2. Please ensure that the email address you provided during online pre-registration is active and accessible. All admission concerns will be communicated through email. You will receive an autoreply confirming your submitted application, which will serve as proof of your online pre-registration. .
  3. Online pre-registration is open until April 07, 2025
Onsite

Please see www.pnc.edu.ph/campus-safety before visiting.

  1. Visit the PNC campus during office hours.
  2. Submit the Initial requirements. The application will be accepted starting January 27, 2025 until April 07, 2025. Physical copies of the requirements should be submitted to the Admissions Department.
  3. Only pre-registered applicants who have submitted the initial requirements within the given period will be scheduled for the assessment examination. Details regarding the schedule of the assessment examination will be discussed by the Admissions Department upon submission of the initial requirements.
  4. Prepare the necessary admission requirements for submission on or before the start of classes.
